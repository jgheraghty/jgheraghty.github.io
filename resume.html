Resumé

Introduction

My name is Joanne Heraghty. I'm a recent graduate from the Institute of Technology, Sligo. I have a strong interest in technology, especially in graphics, web and UX design.


This is evident in my recent internship with Easy Payments Plus/MyEasyPay, and in many of the projects I have undertaken.


Working as a front-end graphics designer with Easy Payments Plus/ MyEasyPay enabled me to demonstrate and improve my design skills as well as offering me an invaluable opportunity to work and collaborate within a professional setting.


Outside of work, my interests in technology extend from design to general computing. I enjoy participating in small projects and voluntary roles where I tutor or assist others with general computer tasks. In my spare time I maintain a blog on my current projects.


My experience not only focuses on design. I have a background in teaching. I have taught web design, mind-games, strategies and code-breaking, and currently I am teaching Gizmos and Gadgets.


Finally, I spend a large portion of my time encouraging others to pursue an interest in technology, with a focus on women in technology. This is visible in my volunteer work in 121Digital, promoting the IT Sligo Computing Department on Open Days on campus and similar events, and hosting summer camps. I am also a member of the Associate Computing in Machinery (ACM), and a member of Sligo's technology MeetUp group that hosts technology events and hackathons.
